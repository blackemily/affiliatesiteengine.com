FAQ
Why keep it limited?

Because the production system is a full build engine. The preview exists to prove judgment and architecture thinking without giving away the factory (full blueprint, internal linking, and batch HTML generation).

Do I need keywords or word counts?

No. The system uses intent-based sizing and semantic topic clusters. No keyword stuffing, no density games.

Is this for beginners or experienced marketers?

Both. Beginners get clarity and a working structure. Experienced marketers use it to validate niches and avoid wasted builds.

Does this guarantee rankings or income?

No. This tool produces real assets and coherent structure. Results depend on niche, competition, publishing, and patience.
